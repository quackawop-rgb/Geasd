# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want the Geasd community to be open, welcoming, and respectful. We pledge to make participation in our project and community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

---

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful and constructive
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Accepting constructive criticism gracefully
- Focusing on what is best for the community and project
- Showing empathy toward other community members

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, trolling, insults, or personal attacks
- Discriminatory jokes, language, or imagery
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Spam, self-promotion, or off-topic flooding
- Misrepresenting the Geasd mod, its author, or official downloads
- Encouraging others to download unofficial or tampered copies of the mod

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all community spaces related to Geasd, including:

- [GitHub Issues](https://github.com/quackawop-rgb/Geasd/issues) and pull requests
- [GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/quackawop-rgb/Geasd) (if enabled)
- The [Geasd Wiki](https://github.com/quackawop-rgb/Geasd/wiki)
- [Modrinth](https://modrinth.com/mod/geasd) comments and reviews
- Any other channels officially linked to this project

It also applies when someone is representing the project in public spaces.

---

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior. They may remove, edit, or reject contributions — including issues, comments, and wiki edits — that violate this Code of Conduct.

Maintainers may temporarily or permanently ban any contributor for behavior they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.

---

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it to the repository owner:

- Via [GitHub](https://github.com/quackawop-rgb) (private message if possible)
- Or through a private security/vulnerability report only if the issue involves a security concern

Please include:

- What happened
- Where it happened (GitHub, Modrinth, etc.)
- When it happened
- Any relevant links or screenshots

All reports will be reviewed and handled with discretion.
